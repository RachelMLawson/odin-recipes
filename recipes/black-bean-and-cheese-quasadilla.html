Black Bean and Cheese Quasadilla

Description

A quick and easy lunch packed with hearty deliciousness.

Ingredients

  • 1 whole wheat tortilla
  • 2 cups shredded Mexican cheese
  • 1 cup cooked black beans
  • 1/2 tbsp olive oil

Steps

  1. Warm a frying pan on medium heat. Lay a tortilla in the pan flat, flipping it over after about 30 seconds.
  2. On one half of the tortilla, sprinkle the cheese and black beans in an even layer.
  3. Fold the tortilla in half, pressing down with a spatula to make the tortilla edges meet like an envelope.
  4. Brush some olive oil on the top side of the quasadilla, then flip over and brush the other side.
  5. Let cook until the bottom side starts to brown and the cheese melts, then flip over and do the same on the opposite side.
  6. Remove quasadilla from pan and let cool, then cut into slices and serve.
