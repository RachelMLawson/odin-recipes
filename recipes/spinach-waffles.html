Baby Spinach Waffles

Description

These spinach waffles are easy to make, freezer-friendly, and a great way to sneak some veggies into your picky baby or toddler's diet.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups whole wheat flour
  • 1 tbsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1/4 cup applesauce
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 cup spinach

Steps

  1. Preheat waffle iron on medium.
  2. In a large bowl, stir together flour, baking powder, cinnamon, and salt.
  3. Blend eggs, milk, oil, applesauce, vanilla extract, and spinach in a blender. Make sure to completely blend spinach.
  4. Add spinach mixture to dry ingredients bowl and combine.
  5. Pour waffle batter into waffle iron in small increments, cooking until slightly golden on the edges.
  6. Serve or store in the fridge or freezer for later.
