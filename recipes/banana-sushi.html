Banana Sushi

Description

This is an easy snack that's perfect for bento boxes and a sure fire toddler pleaser.

Ingredients

  • 1 banana
  • 1 tbsp peanut butter
  • 1 whole wheat tortilla

Steps

  1. Lay a tortilla flat and spread with a thin layer of peanut butter.
  2. Place a peeled banana on the edge of the tortilla and roll it up, being careful not to break or smash the banana.
  3. Slice the wrapped banana into sushi-sized pieces.
  4. Serve or pack in a bento box for later.
